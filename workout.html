Workout Schedule with Videos

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

  • Chest Press - 3 sets of 10-12 reps. You should not be able to do more than 15 reps with the weight. If you can, increase the weight.
  • Incline Chest Press - 3 sets of 10-12 reps You should not be able to do more than 15 reps with the weight. If you can, increase the weight.
  • Pec Deck Machine - 2 sets of 10-12 reps , squeezing the muscle with medium weight.
  • Triceps Push Down - 3 sets of 10-12 reps You should not be able to do more than 15 reps with the weight. If you can, increase the weight.
  • Triceps Overhead Extension - 3 sets of 10-12 reps You should not be able to do more than 15 reps with the weight. If you can, increase the weight.
